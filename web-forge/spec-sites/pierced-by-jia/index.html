About Jia

A calm studio presence backed by real hands-on training.

Jia never planned on becoming a piercer. What started as curiosity turned into a certification at Primal Instinct Body Piercing School in New Hampshire — hands-on training in anatomy, sterilization, placement, and technique built on precision, safety, and respect for the craft.

The goal is never just the piercing itself. It’s how the placement sits, how the client feels during the appointment, and how confidently they leave the chair.

Professionalism first

Clients consistently cite cleanliness, privacy, proper PPE, and sterile equipment opened in front of them as defining strengths.

Personal, not transactional

Every client leaves knowing how their piercing was placed, how to care for it, and exactly what healing should feel like.

“I’m not here to just poke holes. I’m here to help people feel confident, expressive, and a little more like themselves.”

What To Expect

Every appointment runs the same way: clear, steady, and at your pace.

  1. 01

    Consultation

    Start with placement goals, anatomy, comfort level, and the kind of jewelry finish you want to wear every day.

  2. 02

    Placement Mapping

    Jia confirms spacing, symmetry, and how the piece will sit so the final look feels intentional before anything begins.

  3. 03

    Sterile Prep

    Every instrument is sterile. Every setup is done in front of you. Hand hygiene, proper PPE, and a clean environment aren't extras — they're standard.

  4. 04

    Precision Piercing

    Communication stays calm, the process stays efficient, and the focus remains on comfort as much as clean execution.

  5. 05

    Aftercare Guidance

    Healing support is part of the value. Clients leave knowing how to care for the piece and what to expect next.
