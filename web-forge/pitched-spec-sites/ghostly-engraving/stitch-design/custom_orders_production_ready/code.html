Artwork Preparation

For the highest fidelity engraving, we recommend vector-based files (SVG, AI, or DXF). High-resolution raster images (300dpi PNG/JPG) are also accepted for photographic etchings. Our master technicians review every file and will reach out if adjustments are required to ensure a flawless burn.
