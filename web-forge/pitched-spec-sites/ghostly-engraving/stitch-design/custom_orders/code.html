Artwork Preparation

For the highest quality engraving, we recommend vector files (SVG or AI). If you only have a raster image, ensure it is high-resolution (300dpi) and in high-contrast black and white. Don't worry—our studio team reviews every file and will reach out if adjustments are needed.

Transparent Artisanal Pricing

We believe in fair, clear pricing. Your quote will include material costs, setup fees, and engraving time with no hidden surcharges.
