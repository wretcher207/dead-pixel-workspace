Our Story

Rooted in Community

Lavender Puff is a piece of Caribou. For years, this has been where friends catch up, confidence is restored, and taking care of yourself is just what you do.

Recently refreshed with updated aesthetics, expanded education, and refined techniques. But the thing that makes this place work hasn't changed: the warmth. No big-city pretension. Skilled stylists, good products, and genuine care for the people who walk through the door.

Whether you've been coming here for a decade or you're walking in for the first time, you're our guest. Sit down, have a coffee, and let us handle the rest.

Beauty should never feel intimidating. We believe in soft enhancements, protecting the health of your hair, and providing a space where you actually relax.
