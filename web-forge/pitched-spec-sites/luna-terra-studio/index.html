Barre, pilates, mobility, and release on the fourth floor of Dana Warp Mill. Big windows, wood floors, donation classes most of the week, and a schedule that keeps growing.

What We Do

Three ways to
use the room.

Weekly classes

Barre, pilates sculpt, myofascial rolling, and midday mindfulness. Most sessions run 45 minutes. Donation-based this season while we build out the schedule.

Workshops

Guest-led sessions in breath, sound, dance, and longer-form mobility. New workshops get added to the calendar every month.

Book the studio

Private classes, photo and video shoots, small pop-ups, and birthday movement parties. Rent the room by the hour. Email us what you're planning.
