The person behind the products

Julia is a licensed esthetician and MIXI Certified Acne Professional. She graduated from Spa Tech Institute with a degree in health sciences and holistic health, which means she understands skin from the inside out. She opened SkinShift Studio because she wanted to build a practice around actual results, not retail markup.

Her approach is rooted in Korean skincare philosophy: barrier health first, aggressive treatments second. She specializes in sensitive and acne-prone skin because those are the clients who have been burned by bad advice the most. Every treatment plan is built on what the science says, not what sounds luxurious on a menu.

Clients come back because Julia is honest. She will tell you what your skin needs, what it does not need, and what you can do at home for free. Her space is clean, calm, and pressure-free. You are not a ticket number. You are a person with a face, and she pays attention to it.
