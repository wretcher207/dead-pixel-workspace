about the place
"We opened because Biddeford needed a place to be weird and well-fed at the same time."

It started back in 2019 with a single-group roaster, a questionable amount of homemade hazelnut syrup, and a very loud radiator. We didn't have a plan so much as we had a persistent craving for better coffee and less pretense.

And yes, Nettie is a real person. You’ll usually find her behind the counter perfecting the morning bun—a pastry that takes three days to make and about three seconds to disappear.
