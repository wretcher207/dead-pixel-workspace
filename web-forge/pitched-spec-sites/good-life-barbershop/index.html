About the Shop

A neighborhood
barbershop, done right.

Good Life Barbershop sits on Broadway in South Portland. Walk in and you'll notice the details. Warm decor, good music, a clean shop that actually feels welcoming.

Shelby opened Good Life with a simple idea: build a space where every person who sits in the chair leaves feeling better than when they walked in. No attitude. No rush. Just skilled barbers who listen.

Walk-ins are always welcome. Appointments through Fresha if you want to lock in a time.

What We Do

Beard Trim
