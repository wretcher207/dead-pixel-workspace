While you're in town

Make a morning
of it.

Grab a bagel and a coffee, then walk a block down Main toward the water. The harbor is right there. The breakwater and its lighthouse are a short drive north. The Farnsworth Art Museum is around the corner, and the ferry to Vinalhaven leaves from the terminal fifteen minutes down the road.

In summer, bring your bagel and sit on the public benches by the harbor park. It's the best breakfast spot in town, and it didn't even cost you a reservation.

Do you take orders ahead?

For a dozen or more, call by the day before — (207) 594-3600. For smaller orders, just come in.

What time should I get there?

Before 10am for the full board. Specials usually go first. After 11am, the racks start thinning out.

Gluten-free?

Not yet — we only bake wheat bagels. Good Tern upstairs carries gluten-free bread and pastries.

Can I bring a dog?

Well-behaved dogs are welcome at the outdoor picnic tables in warm weather.
