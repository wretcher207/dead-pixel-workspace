What To Expect

How every appointment works.

  1. 01

    Consultation

    Talk through what you want, where it's going, and what jewelry you're thinking. Jia checks anatomy and comfort before anything else.

  2. 02

    Placement Mapping

    Spacing, symmetry, and how the piece sits get confirmed before anything starts. You see the placement first.

  3. 03

    Sterile Prep

    All equipment is opened in front of you. Hand hygiene, proper PPE, and a sterile environment. Standard, not optional.

  4. 04

    Precision Piercing

    Quick, steady, and calm. Jia keeps you in the loop the whole time so nothing catches you off guard.

  5. 05

    Aftercare Guidance

    You leave knowing exactly how to clean it, what to watch for, and what normal healing looks like.
