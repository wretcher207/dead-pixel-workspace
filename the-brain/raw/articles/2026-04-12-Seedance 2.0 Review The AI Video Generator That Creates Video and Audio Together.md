## Seedance 2.0 vs Previous Models: What Changed?

Seedance 1.0 (June 2025) launched as a strong text-to-video and image-to-video model. Its standout feature was prompt accuracy — it delivered what you actually described, including complex multi-scene narratives that most models struggled with.

Seedance 1.5 Pro (December 2025) introduced native audio-visual joint generation. Most AI video tools still separate video and audio into two different workflows. Seedance 1.5 Pro merged them. Seedance 2.0 extends this foundation with stronger motion coherence, more reliable handling of complex scene descriptions, and improved visual fidelity. Seedance 1.5 Pro sits in the global top 15 for image-to-video at approximately $1.56 per minute.
