### Is Seedance 2.0 Overhyped?

Below, we dive into our honest review of [Seedance 2.0](https://seed.bytedance.com/en/seedance2_0) and share examples from our testing process.

#### What Is Seedance 2.0?

Seedance is an AI video model developed by ByteDance. If that name sounds familiar, it’s because they are the powerhouses behind TikTok. While Seedance 1.0 briefly held the crown as one of the strongest generators on the market, the space evolved so quickly that it was soon eclipsed by newer models.

[Seedance 2.0](https://seed.bytedance.com/en/seedance2_0) feels like a massive return to form. It’s a major step forward in visual quality, offering features that push way beyond basic text-to-video.

**The new platform includes:**

- **Multi-shot scene generation:** Create cohesive sequences.
- **Built-in lip syncing:** For more realistic character performances.
- **Sound effects and music:** An all-in-one audio-visual output.
- **Improved camera movement:** More dynamic cinematic control.
- **Stronger physics:** Objects feel grounded and real.

The most exciting part? You aren't just generating single clips anymore; you can build entire cinematic sequences with visual continuity.

### Is Seedance 2.0 the End of Hollywood?

The short answer? No.

The narrative that AI is replacing the entire film industry is massively exaggerated. Seedance 2.0 is a powerful tool, but it currently lacks the resolution, legal reliability, and professional stability required for major studio productions.

The quality is still not where it would need to be to be used on a professional project, but it does seem to be the best model yet!
