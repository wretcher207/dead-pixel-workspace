# Claude AI for Small Business: What It Does, How It Compares to ChatGPT, and Why It Matters

## Summary
Cristina Popov's guide positions [[claude]] as a tool optimized for small business document work and analysis, with strengths in clarity and handling long-form content. Built by [[anthropic]] using [[constitutional-ai]], Claude excels where ChatGPT is weaker: contract analysis, research summarization, customer feedback synthesis, and precise explanations. Small business use cases lean toward content refinement, document summarization, template creation, and trend analysis rather than creative ideation.

## Key Claims
- [[claude]] created by [[anthropic]] (founded by former OpenAI researchers) using [[constitutional-ai]] for ethical reasoning and safety
- [[anthropic]] recently changed privacy stance: user interactions may now be used to train future models (was previously "no training on user data")
- [[claude]] excels at: reading long documents, clear explanations, data analysis; ChatGPT stronger at: creative writing, brainstorming, tool integrations
- Free plan available; Pro plan $20/month (similar to ChatGPT Plus); Team plan ~$25-30/user/month
- Context limit very large; can handle long files where ChatGPT hits limits more quickly
- [[constitutional-ai]] approach makes [[claude]] more likely to refuse harmful requests; personality less adaptable than ChatGPT
- Small business applications: content improvement, document summarization, customer feedback analysis, brainstorming, template/procedure creation, translation, research synthesis

## Notable Details
- Small business sweet spot: consultants, coaches, agencies, online store owners (work with lots of written content)
- Example use: paste blog draft, ask for conciseness + friendliness improvements + three quick tips
- Example: upload customer reviews, ask for top three complaints and improvement suggestions
- Can analyze call transcripts, compare document versions, clean and transform datasets, generate charts
- Example: "Summarize the top three complaints and what we could improve" from customer feedback
- Safety built-in but requires human verification: Claude can hallucinate or make mistakes; always fact-check before publishing
- GDPR awareness: review privacy policy before uploading personal data; don't upload HIPAA or PII without understanding legal implications
- Best practices: be specific in requests, give examples of tone/style, ask follow-ups, build prompt library, track time savings as ROI measure
