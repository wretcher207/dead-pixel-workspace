# Writing Style Sample

**Source:** `raw/notes/Writing.docx`
**Format:** Opening monologue / voice sample for autobiographical project

## Summary

The opening salvo of David's writing project. Addresses the reader directly, warns them what they're getting into, and establishes the voice: profane, self-aware, darkly funny, brutally honest. Sets up the central premise — this is all true, it's all ugly, and he's telling it anyway.

## Key Claims / Themes

- "I truly believe a pathway to clear and cold consciousness is [[suffering-as-pathway|suffering]]." — the philosophical anchor of the entire project
- David describes himself as someone who tries to be as invisible as possible — a game of how unseen he can make himself (see [[invisibility]])
- The truth is the selling point. Everything is real. Names changed, nothing else.
- The absurdity of his living situation is a recurring comedic device — coaching [[lou]] on AI prompting while a transsexual from high school does laundry upstairs, living in [[alyssa]]'s basement
- Self-deprecating but not self-pitying. There's a distinction. He invites the reader to feel better about their own life, not to feel sorry for his.

## Voice Characteristics

- Second-person address to the reader ("You're gonna think I'm full of shit")
- Stream of consciousness with sharp pivots
- Profanity as punctuation, not shock value
- Dark humor as coping mechanism and storytelling tool
- Tangential asides that circle back to the point
- Capital letters for emphasis over italics

## Notable Quotes

> "You're gonna think I'm full of shit. I don't blame you."

> "I truly believe a pathway to clear and cold consciousness is suffering."

> "Life is strange."
