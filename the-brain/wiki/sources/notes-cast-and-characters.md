# Cast and Characters

**Source:** `raw/notes/Cast and characters.docx`
**Format:** Character reference sheet for David's autobiographical writing project

## Summary

A roster of the key people in David's life, written in his own voice. No filter, no softening. Each entry is a raw, compressed biography — who they are, what they did, why they matter. Functions as a dramatis personae for the larger [[writing-project]].

## Key People Introduced

- **[[jo]]** — David's mother. Had him at 18. Relationship oscillates between best friends and not speaking. Reconnected through drugs and alcohol after David's divorce. Mental health declining.
- **[[dad]]** — David's father (Dave J.R.). Lives in CT. Incarcerated early in David's childhood for drugs. Cleaned up, remarried, ended up with custody of David's son David IV. Currently not speaking.
- **[[annie]]** — Girlfriend/ex-girlfriend. They have a child together. David describes her as mentally unwell, likely bipolar with antisocial personality disorder. Central figure in much of the writing.
- **[[lou]]** — Mother of David's high school ex-girlfriend [[alyssa]]. Unlikely friendship. David lives in their house. She comes to him for advice.
- **[[alyssa]]** — High school ex-girlfriend, first love. Gave David a place to stay when [[jo]] threw him out. David considers her his best friend.
- **[[rory]]** — [[annie]]'s father. Gay, ex-con, former meth dealer. David sees a direct line between Rory and Annie's dysfunction.

## Notable Patterns

- Almost every relationship is marked by substance abuse, incarceration, or mental illness
- The word "toxic" appears repeatedly — David is aware of the patterns, not blind to them
- Despite the chaos, genuine love runs through several relationships (Jo, Alyssa)
- David IV's custody situation spans multiple entries — a wound that crosses generations
