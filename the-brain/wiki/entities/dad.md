# Dad

AKA: Dave J.R., Pops. David's father.

## Profile

- Lives in Connecticut
- Incarcerated for drugs during David's early childhood — wasn't around much
- Cleaned up his act, married a good woman (around when David was ~14)
- Eventually received custody of David IV (David's son) when the boy's mother gave him up

## Relationship with [[david]]

- Currently not speaking
- The absence during childhood is a foundational wound
- The custody of David IV is complicated — David lost his son, and his father is now raising him

## Role in the Writing

Less prominent than [[jo]] or [[annie]] in the existing source material. But the generational pattern is there: Dave J.R. was incarcerated, absent, then cleaned up. David's own trajectory echoes parts of this. Three generations of David Russells, each trying to break the cycle or at least survive it.
