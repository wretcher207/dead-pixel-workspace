# Alyssa

David's high school ex-girlfriend. [[lou]]'s daughter. Current housemate. The person David considers his closest friend.

## Profile

- First love
- The relationship ended in high school when she got caught selling "hillbilly heroin" — trying to fit in with the wrong crowd
- David was into the hardcore/straight edge scene at the time and was completely unaware. She hid it from him knowing how he'd react.
- They've stayed in touch most of their lives since

## Relationship with [[david]]

- Gave him a place to stay when [[jo]] threw him out
- David lives in her basement, next door to [[lou]]
- David considers her his best friend — "out of anyone"
- "Kinda crazy but solid" — high praise in David's world
- "Has done a tremendous amount for me"

## Role in the Writing

Alyssa is the counterweight to [[annie]]. Where Annie destroys, Alyssa shelters. Where Annie lies, Alyssa is solid. She represents the people who showed up when it mattered — the "absolute angels" David references in the writing style sample. The irony of ending up in his first love's basement isn't lost on him.
