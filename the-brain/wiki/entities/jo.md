# Jo

AKA: Mom, Mother, JoJo, Joanne. David's mother.

## Profile

- Had David at 18, was pregnant through her graduation
- Went to a Metallica and Ozzy concert with David in the womb
- History of hard drugs and alcohol
- Mental health deteriorating with age

## Relationship with [[david]]

- David hated her for most of his life — how he was raised, the job she did
- After his divorce, he moved back in with her. They reconnected, but "in a different way through drugs and alcohol"
- Toxic relationship with enabling on both sides
- Oscillates: talking one week, not the next
- Threw David out during "a benzo and booze fueled rampage" in mid-October (year unspecified), which led to him moving in with [[alyssa]]

## Role in the Writing

Jo represents the generational pattern — addiction, instability, love that coexists with destruction. David's feelings about her are the most conflicted in the cast. She's "my dear dear Mother" in the same sentence as an indictment.
