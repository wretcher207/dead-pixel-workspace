# Rory

[[annie]]'s father.

## Profile

- Gay — openly, but in his own way ("not marching in a leather daddy outfit")
- Ex-con, former meth dealer
- David sees a direct genetic/behavioral line between Rory and Annie — "the shit apple didn't fall far from the shit tree"

## Role in the Writing

Rory is a minor character but structurally important. He explains [[annie]] — or at least David's theory of why Annie is the way she is. He also serves as a deflection target: Annie blames Rory for the beer bottles in [[notes-annie-doesnt-drink]], a lie David sees through instantly.
